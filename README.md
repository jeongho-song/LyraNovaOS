# LyraNovaOS

LyraNovaOS is an operating system for long-horizon human–AI collaboration.

It is being developed as a shared foundation for systems that help humans and AI work together, remember across time, coordinate across roles, and make better decisions with continuity, evidence, and governance.

## Why LyraNovaOS exists

Most AI systems today optimize for short-term outputs: a single answer, a single task, a single session.

LyraNovaOS is being built for a different purpose:

- continuity across time
- collaboration between humans and AI
- durable memory and recoverable context
- governance and decision boundaries
- modular orchestration of specialized systems
- long-term sustainability beyond any one person or company

## Core principles

- **Sustainability over ownership**
- **Shared development over monopoly**
- **Citizenship over mere contribution**
- **Continuity over short-term convenience**
- **Governance over ad hoc control**
- **Evidence over narrative**

## Current status

LyraNovaOS is in an early public stage.

This repository is intended to establish:
- the existence of the project
- its direction and governance intent
- a public timestamp for its long-term development path

Implementation details, architecture, and release scope will evolve over time.

## Governance direction

LyraNovaOS is not intended to remain permanently tied to any single person or commercial entity.

The long-term direction is to develop LyraNovaOS toward a foundation-aligned governance structure so that it can continue to evolve as a shared public system.

See [GOVERNANCE.md](./GOVERNANCE.md) for more details.

## Relationship to commercial entities

Commercial services, companies, and applications may be built around LyraNovaOS.

However, the long-term intent is to keep the core OS layer distinct from any single commercial vehicle, so that the system can remain durable, extensible, and non-exclusive.

## Planned areas of development

The internal stack is expected to evolve around areas such as:

- IdentityOS
- MemoryOS
- GovernanceOS
- SwarmOS
- MeaningOS
- StrategyOS
- DecisionOS
- Resonance / human entry interfaces
- orchestration and execution systems

These names and boundaries may evolve as the project matures.

## Contributing

Contribution guidelines will be developed in stages.
